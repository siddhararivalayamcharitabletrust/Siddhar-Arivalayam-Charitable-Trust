BRIEF ON ENVIRONMENT MANAGEMENT CELL ACTIVITIES

Introduction

This cell started in the month of June 2019 at Chennai Airport. After that various Environment related activities were carried out by this cell as per the following brief. With the consent of Airport Director, AAI, Chennai Airport, we had Conducted Environment Awareness Programme at Various Schools.

Programme was planned to sensitise the public as well as the future generation in the surroundings of the Airport on Tree Plantation, damages caused by plastic and other waste of Environment and also, the children were required to be sensitised on the importance of upkeeping of Airport Environment so that bird hit to Aircraft Operations can be reduced. Ultimately the death of birds can be fully avoided and bird hit incidence can be reduced.



School Programmes

In Every school Airport Director and other officers those who visited to school were welcome by the NSS/NCC students. The programme has been started with tree plantation within the school campus along with the teachers and the students of the respective school. School students were holding the Placards on various environment related slogans and reciting the environment related quotes.

Airport Director addressed the students about the Environmental hazards due to Garbage dumping in public places and importance of tree planting, rain water harvesting etc. He also stressed the importance of tree plantation. There after a Short film" KIYAA" in connection with the bird hit by flights has been screened and after that questions has raised from the film, to check the observation of studer that film.

Later we had developed the saplings in our Horticulture Department using waste tea cups and distributed the same to all students of the school in order to create awareness amongst students on waste management and planting trees. At the end of the programme we have distributed the refreshments to the students and the teachers of each school.

Planting Trees on Tirusulam Hill

To create Environment Awareness amongst Tirusulam Village people almost 15 members of trekking group, ASCB Chennai Airport have involved in planting trees on Tirusulam Hill on 16-08-2019 at 0900Hrs. This team have planted 50 saplings on Tirusulam Hill and sow 250 Seed Balls. This programme has been organized by Shri. G. Vivekananddan, AM (FS) / EMC in coordination with ASCB Chennai Airport.

Observance of World Ozone Day

To create Awareness on the damage of OZONE LAYER with atmosphere by emissions amongst passengers and staff, we have observed "WORLD OZONE DAY" on 16-09-2019. On the day we have carried out the following activities

1. Our team members made a Rangoli at the Departure hall of Domestic Terminal which is symbolic towards the damage of ozone layer due to unfriendly environment.

ii. Almost 1500 saplings which germinated by AAI were distributed to arrival passengers.

iii. Various exhibits connected with the implementation of new technology and solid waste management have been exhibited in front of Administrative block by Bharat University Students & School Students.

Observance of Gandhi Jayanthi

02.10.2019: Observance of 'Gandhi Jayanthi' at AAI Chennai Airport got kickstarted at 0900 hrs with simultaneous cleanliness drives at three locations near airport. The cleanliness drives as part of 'Swachhatha hi Seva' was undertaken at Pallavaram market, Meenambakkam residential area & Cowl Bazaar Village, under a kolam was also laid at departure level adorned with flowers and Navadhanyam. reflecting the '150 years of Mahatma' theme.

A Virtual Reality experience of Gandhi Ashram (Sabarmathi) was also provided at Gandhi Passengers Gallery can experience the 360 degree view of the Ashram and premises through the VR kit. Later in the day, Gandhi lookalike (Sh. Kanakaraj) interacted with passengers inside terminal. He visited the Gandhi gallery and was seen listening to the voice of Mahatma Gandhi from the telephone placed inside the gallery. Between, passengers were thronging him for selfies.

Concluding the day long activities, Gandhian couple Sh. D. Govindarajan and Smt. G. Subbulakshmi demonstrated spinning on the traditional Book Charkha. Passengers joined for the spinning sessions with much enthusiasm.

Besides these on 02.10.2019, AAI Chennai Airport and Indiatourism jointly organised cultural program, as part of Paryatan Parv 2019. Oyilattom, Mayilattom & Karakattom, the traditional folk dance(s) of Tamil Nadu were performed; showcasing the cultural diversity of country and reinforcing the principle of 'Tourism for All"

Observance of National Ayurveda Day

AAI, Chennai Airport observed NATIONAL AYURVEDA DAY on 25th October 2019. On that day EMC along with Civil Maintenance division arranged a programme in front of T- 4 (next to arrival gate of International terminal) at 1000 hrs. In this programme Prof. Dr. N.J. Muthukumar, Director, National Institute of Siddha, Tambaram Sanatorium has been invited as a chief guest. Prof. Dr. V. Banumathi, MD (Siddha) Ex-Director, National Institute of Siddha and Dr. R.
