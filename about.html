Siddhar Arivalayam Charitable Trust



Location:

Nestled at the foot of Javvadhu Malai, one of the majestic mountains in the Eastern Ghats of Tamil Nadu, Siddhar Arivalayam Charitable Trust operates with divine guidance and a commitment to preserving ancient wisdom and nature.


Foundation:

Our trust was established under the divine guidance of “Om Agasthiyar Maa Perumaan,” the principal disciple of Om Shiva Perumaan. Om Agasthiyar Maa Perumaan is revered as the Guru of all diviners, including Siddhas, Saints, Priests, Yogis, Saadhus, and various other forms of diviners throughout the universe. He is credited with designing our lifestyle, the Tamil language, literature, and imparting all the knowledge known to humankind.


Mission:

Our mission is to conserve the natural and cultural heritage of the Javvadhu Malai and all over the globe, promoting sustainable practices and holistic well-being through traditional wisdom integrated with modern initiatives.


Key Initiatives:


1. Javvadhu Sanjeevi Malai Girivalam:

Sanjeevi Malai, located at the crown of Javvadhu Malai, is a high-powerful site with temples of Om Hanuman and Om Shiva Perumaan. - The region is rich in life-saving herbs and offers a Girivalam path, encouraging visitors to walk around the hill, inhaling herbal air for health and divine blessings. - This initiative aims to conserve the Sanjeevi Forest and its ancient trees, protecting it from deforestation and other harmful practices.


2. Mooligai Thiri – Herbal Wick:

Handmade by village elders, these herbal wicks are crafted from highly divine herb called Jyothi Izhai. - Lighting these wicks with pure Iluppai oil (Mahua oil) removes negative energies, promoting a harmonious environment.


3. Kuliyal Mooligaigal Herbal Bath Dips:

Following our Ancestral tradition, The Om Agasthiyar herbal bath powder is made from ten different highly efficient and divinely herbs, providing user-friendly herbal steam and bath experiences.


4. Karuppu Kavuni Arisi:

Known as the "King" of all rice varieties, this ancient black rice helps in reducing obesity, aging, cancer, diabetes, and improving overall health.


5. Rudraaksha Parikaram:

A divinely secret Siddha's practice of putting Rudraaksha beads in ant-hills to transform areas into sacred Shiva Boomi, to balance our karma.Hence Let's practice this to support global peace.




6. Elephant Rearing:

Based on the principle that elephants contribute to forest making, this initiative aims to balance elephant population all over the globe. - The program provides livelihood for families involved in elephant care.Let's donate elephants.


7. Planting & Growing Palm Trees on the Indian Coastline:

Aiming to combat coastal erosion and natural disasters, this project involves planting palm trees along India’s coastline fron West Bengal to Gujarat, which provides long-term protection and resilience.


8. Mei Gnana Panchakshara:

The practice of Mei Gnana Panchakshara (SIVAYANAMA) activates the neurons of our brain leads us to be wisdomic being in the globe. - We work to inscribe these sacred stones in temples, preserving the highest form of knowledge for future generations.


9. Mouna Girivalam:

Mouna Girivalam is a deeply spiritual practice of circumambulating the sacred Arunachala Hill in silence and devotion. This journey is a path of self-reflection, serenity, and connection with the divine. Walking this sacred path is believed to bring immense spiritual benefits and inner peace.


Support and Participation:

We invite you to join us in our efforts to conserve nature, promote health, and preserve ancient wisdom for the prosperity of future generations. Your support and participation in our initiatives will help us achieve our mission of growing Tamil and flourishing righteousness.
