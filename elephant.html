யானை மேய்த்தல் – Elephant Rearing




No one can feed an elephant, due to very high quantity of food it takes. So, this is an idea of growing an elephant by taking it to the forest, for its food requirement and bringing back to the place, at the temple located in the foot hills. The maintenance work and cost for the elephant is very high, which includes proper medicinal facilities, hygiene, Breeding time precautions and all activities associated with this Elephant Rearing. Earlier, the elephants were used in King’s army and temples and was maintained by rulers. But now a days the elephants are present in very old ancient and traditional temples. Elephant has the ability to receive the universal energy at the highest. This is a symbol of pride, as the kings and queens used Elephants during functions and other occasions. The Deity of god is also carried by elephants during festivals. Donating an elephant to the temple, helps this cause of balancing the elephant population and also our wealth grows huge as elephant.
