Meignana panchakshara

The word SIVAYANAMA is termed as Meignana panchakshara. SI represents Fire, VA means Vayu – Air, YA means Sky, NA means Land and MA means water. All the thoughts, actions and any form of Physical materials has combination of these five elements, in various proportions and combinations. Regular Practice of this Meignana panchakshara, given to us by TAMILAANDAVAR OM AGASTHIYAR MAAPERUMAAN, activates the neurons in our brain, increases our cerebral capacity, enables us to be a wisdomic and enlightened being of this globe and Universe. We are working to place the stone inscriptions, in all the temples for prosperity of our generations to come.

Healing Energy
